Emergency Dept Leveling

*Per AMA E/M guidelines, time is not a descriptive component for Emergency Department levels. Codes are leveled solely on MDM.

Modifier Quick Reference

25 E/M

Significant, Separately Identifiable Evaluation and Management Service by the Same Physician on the Same Day of the Procedure or Other Service.

24 E/M

Unrelated Evaluation and Management Service by the Same Physician During a Postoperative Period.

59 Procedure

Distinct Procedural Service. Indicates that a procedure or service was distinct or independent from other non-E/M services performed on the same day.

XE CMS / X-Mod

Separate Encounter. A service that is distinct because it occurred during a separate encounter.

XS CMS / X-Mod

Separate Structure. A service that is distinct because it was performed on a separate organ/structure.

XP CMS / X-Mod

Separate Practitioner. A service that is distinct because it was performed by a different practitioner.

XU CMS / X-Mod

Unusual Non-Overlapping Service. A service that is distinct because it does not overlap usual components of the main service.

26 Professional

Professional Component. Used when reporting only the physician's interpretation/work.
